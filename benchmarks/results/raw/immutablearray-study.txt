# ImmutableArray improvement study: 10,000,000 rows, 20,000-change batch, 15 cycles per variant

| Variant | Apply p50 | Alloc/cycle¹ | Gen2 collections | Caveat |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Naive ToBuilder/ToImmutable | 70 ms | 305.2 MiB | 9 | fresh LOH array every cycle |
| Uninitialized alloc + AsImmutableArray | 18 ms | 152.6 MiB | 5 | still 1 LOH alloc/cycle |
| Builder + MoveToImmutable (safe) | 36 ms | 152.6 MiB | 5 | 1 LOH alloc/cycle; the safe way to avoid the second copy |
| Extract (AsArray) + mutate in place | 0 ms | 0.0 MiB | 0 | NO atomicity: readers see half-applied batches mid-write |
| Pooled double-buffer + wrap | 18 ms | 0.0 MiB | 0 | UNSAFE past 1 cycle: held snapshots get mutated; growth reallocates |
| SnapshotTable.ApplyChanges (reference) | 64 ms | 61.6 MiB | 3 | none — designed for this |
